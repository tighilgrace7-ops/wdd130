Benue

Benue State is located in the North Central region of Nigeria. It is often called the "Food Basket of the Nation" because of its rich agricultural production. The state capital is Makurdi, and the Benue River flows through the state. Benue is known for farming, beautiful landscapes, and diverse cultures.
